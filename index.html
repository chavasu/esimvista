What eSIM is

Plain-English foundation

An eSIM is a digital SIM embedded in a compatible device. Instead of swapping a plastic card, you install a profile and activate a plan digitally. That makes eSIM particularly useful for travel, comparison shopping, and fast setup before a flight.

Use eSIM, What Is eSIM, and How eSIM Works as your core explanatory cluster.

Basics

What is an eSIM?

An eSIM is a digital SIM embedded in a compatible device, allowing activation without a physical SIM card.

How does eSIM work?

You install a carrier profile digitally, then activate it according to the provider's instructions.

Is eSIM the same as a physical SIM?

No. Both connect you to a mobile network, but an eSIM is downloaded while a physical SIM is inserted.

Why do travelers use eSIMs?

They make it easier to buy data online before a trip and avoid airport SIM counters.

Can eSIM replace roaming?

For many trips yes, especially when third-party travel eSIMs are cheaper than carrier roaming.

Compatibility

How do I know if my phone supports eSIM?

Check your phone model, settings menu, and manufacturer support information before buying.

Does every iPhone support eSIM?

Many modern iPhones do, but older models and some region-specific versions may differ.

Does every Android phone support eSIM?

No. Support depends on brand, model, and carrier restrictions.

Do I need an unlocked phone?

In many cases yes, because locked phones may not accept third-party eSIM profiles.

Can tablets use eSIM?

Some tablets can, especially cellular models that list eSIM support.

Buying

Where can I buy an eSIM?

You can buy from official provider websites or apps before you travel.

Is it safe to buy an eSIM online?

It can be, if you buy from reputable providers and verify compatibility first.

When should I buy my eSIM?

Usually before departure, so you can install it while you still have stable internet access.

Should I buy local or regional?

Buy local if one country dominates your trip. Compare regional if you are crossing borders.

What matters more than price?

Compatibility, activation rules, support quality, and whether the plan really matches your usage.

Activation

How do I install an eSIM?

Most providers let you install by QR code or in-app activation.

When does an eSIM start?

That depends on the provider. Some start on installation, others on first network connection.

Can I install an eSIM at home and activate abroad?

Yes, many travelers do exactly that.

Why is my QR code not working?

Common causes include a used code, network issues, incorrect scanning flow, or device restrictions.

Do I need Wi‑Fi to install eSIM?

Usually yes, because your phone needs internet access to download the profile.

Usage

Can I keep WhatsApp with eSIM?

Yes, most people keep using WhatsApp with their existing number while using the eSIM for data.

Can I make calls with a travel eSIM?

Some eSIMs are data-only while others include call or text options.

Can I use hotspot?

Only if the provider and plan allow it.

Can I stream video on eSIM?

Yes, but heavy streaming uses a lot of data, so plan choice matters.

Can I use maps and ride apps with eSIM?

Yes, that is one of the main reasons travelers buy them.

Troubleshooting

Why is my eSIM not connecting?

Check whether the right line is active, whether roaming must be enabled, and whether the APN or network settings need attention.

Should I restart my phone after activation?

Often yes. A restart can help the device register the new line properly.

Why does my phone show signal but no data?

You may need data roaming enabled, a corrected APN, or the correct data line selected.

Can I delete and reinstall an eSIM?

Sometimes, but some providers limit reinstallation, so read the provider terms first.

What if my phone is carrier locked?

A locked phone may block third-party eSIM profiles even if it supports eSIM technically.
